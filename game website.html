Uncharted 3 : Drakes Deception

Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception is an action-adventure video game developed by Naughty Dog and Sony Entertainment. Released in 2011. It follows the adventures of Nathan Drake, a treasure hunter and protagonist of the Uncharted series. In this installment, Nathan sets out on a globe-trotting journey to uncover the truth behind the legendary lost city of Iram of the Pillars, also known as the Atlantis of the Sands.
